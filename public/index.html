The Community Wall

A shared digital mosaic built from drawings uploaded by people everywhere. Different backgrounds. Different styles. One united wall permanently through crypto NFTs.

Innovation & Creativity

In this project, we focused heavily on the concept and creativity behind The Community Wall. We wanted to use blockchain technology in a meaningful way, so we built a system that lets anyone upload an image and mint it as an NFT. Each uploaded image becomes a permanent tile on the Community Wall. It is owned by the creator and preserved forever on the blockchain. Our goal is to give people a place to share their art, interests, and differences while bringing everyone together through a digitalized community wall.
